Anna Currey

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services, where I work on machine translation as part of the Amazon Translate team. Previously, I did my PhD in the Statistical Machine Translation Group at the University of Edinburgh's Institute for Language, Cognition and Computation.

For more information, please see my Google Scholar page or LinkedIn profile.
